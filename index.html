Study Tips

Learning is in fact a pain process despite the fact that physics and math are interesting, but you have to endure the pain in order to absorb the knowledge. So just keep this in mind, when you see others learning faster or better than you does not mean they don't feel this pain, it is just you have not yet get used to it. Thus you do not have to be frustrating when you feel like a dumb, since others also don't have a faster way to master new knowledge, and you just need to endure during the process then you will master some new stuff.In this website, you can find notes on some of the lectures. If you don't feel good, just contact, I can be your tutor!
